Eternal Voices: A Monument of Resistance and Remembrance

An Architectural Design Competition

Introduction

The competition calls for the design of a monument that honors the memory of the 1,000+ lives of students and people lost during the 2024 mass uprising in Bangladesh. This event marks a significant moment in history, reflecting courage, sacrifice, and unyielding spirit.

Objective

Participants are invited to conceptualize a monument that captures the essence of this historic event. The design should create a powerful emotional experience for visitors, symbolizing hope, unity, and the ongoing struggle for justice.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Design Concept & Innovation
  • Aesthetic & Visual Impact
  • Sustainability & Materials
  • Harmonious Proportion
  • Contextuality Integration
  • Accessibility
  • Presentation & Communication

Key Elements to Consider

  • Symbolism
  • Interaction
  • Contextual Sensitivity
  • Materials and Sustainability
  • Accessibility
  • Location
  • Emotional Impact

Registration Details

Registration begins on 25 September, 2024, and ends on 31 October, 2024. The submission deadline is 20 November, 2024.

To register, participants must pay a fee of 2,500 BDT via bKash, Rocket, or Nagad. For more information, visit the official website or contact us via WhatsApp or email.
