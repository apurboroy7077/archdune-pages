Eligibility
  • 01. This competition is open to architecture students only.
    Participation is limited to students currently enrolled in architecture programs at universities or colleges. This ensures that submissions are relevant and informed by current architectural practices and education. Professionals or non-architecture students are not eligible.
  • 02. Individual or Team (Maximum 03 participants) registration.
    Participants can register either as individuals or in teams consisting of up to three members. Collaboration within teams can allow for diverse ideas and approaches to the monument design. Teams are encouraged to manage their roles effectively for a strong submission.
  • 03. A participant may submit a single entry.
    Each participant, whether working alone or in a team, is allowed to submit only one entry. This rule encourages participants to focus their creativity and effort on a single, well-thought-out design. Multiple submissions by the same participant will not be considered.
  • 04. The competition is limited to Bangladesh only.
    The competition is exclusively for individuals and teams residing in Bangladesh. This limitation ensures the monument remains deeply rooted in local context and resonates with the nationâ€™s history. International participants are not eligible to enter.
